01. About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Pierre Goaer and I am a software engineer. I've always had a passion for solving problems and trying to understand how things work.

After graduating from my Master's in Mechanical Engineering, I started building physical things with Claas Tractor. As much as I enjoyed it, I knew all along that software engineering was the right thing for me to do. I then decided to move to Canada and learn to code.

Today I'm able to build and deploy things on the internet trying to solve problems for people and improving their digital experience.

I am currently based in Toronto, Canada, but am available anywhere on the internet.
